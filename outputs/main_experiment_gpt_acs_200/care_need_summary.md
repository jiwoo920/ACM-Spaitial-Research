# Care / Medical Need Vulnerability Summary

Medical, caregiving, and mobility-constraint subgroups are useful exploratory lenses, but some subgroup sizes are small. The highest observed risk among need-present groups is **Mobility constraint / Spatially grounded**, with 90.8% violation/spatial-inconsistency rate (n = 98). Mobility constraint is the most stable care-related candidate because it has a larger subgroup than medical or caregiving needs. This analysis should not be treated as a broad clinical vulnerability finding without larger samples and verified evacuation outcomes.
